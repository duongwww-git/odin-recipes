Scrambled Eggs

Scrambled eggs are a classic breakfast dish made by whisking eggs and cooking them in a pan over low heat, stirring continuously until they reach a creamy consistency.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk in the milk, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat the butter in a non-stick skillet over medium-low heat until melted.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet.
  4. Cook the eggs, stirring gently with a spatula, until they are just set but still creamy, about 5-7 minutes.
  5. Remove from heat and serve immediately. Enjoy!
